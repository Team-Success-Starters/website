overveiwe of our project


Staying connected with alumni has always been a challenge for schools. There is no simple way to keep track of how their graduated students are doing later on in life. Administrators from schools would love to have a system that is able to manage that information.

VCA would like to put in place a customized software application designed to provide an ongoing assessment of student involvement and success during their high school years following graduation from VCA.
